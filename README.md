# 👴🤖 ElderBot

> **An AI-powered companion helping older adults live safer, healthier, and more independent lives.**

---

## 🌟 Overview

**ElderBot** is an AI-powered assistant designed specifically for seniors, combining **voice interaction**, **computer vision**, and **intelligent automation** into one simple and accessible platform.

Our goal is to help older adults confidently manage everyday tasks such as medication reminders, understanding important documents, identifying scams, and contacting caregivers during emergencies.

Built for the **🏆 AMD × Fireworks AI Hackathon 2026**, ElderBot focuses on creating technology that is simple, accessible, and truly impactful.

---

## ✨ Planned Features

- 🎙️ AI Voice Assistant
- 💊 Smart Medication Reminders
- 📄 OCR Document & Prescription Explanation
- 🛡️ Scam Detection
- 🚨 One-Tap SOS Emergency Assistance
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Caregiver Notifications
- ♿ Elder-Friendly Accessible Interface
